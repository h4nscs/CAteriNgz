Our Origin
15 years ago we were founded. It started with Mrs. Okky's hobby of cooking and seeing her son’s friends who often asked for her son’s meals. Mrs. Okky founded CAteriNgz with the intention of providing delicious and nutritious food. CAteriNgz is increasingly growing with order also coming from employees and workers. We are increasingly known to the public because of its delicious taste and variety of menus. With the same intention of providing delicious and nutritious food, CAteriNgz is exploring the wedding catering business.
Our Mission
CAteriNgz's mission is to provide delicious and healthy food for busy people and provide a variety of meals choices to reach various groups, so that everyone can enjoy our masterpieces.
